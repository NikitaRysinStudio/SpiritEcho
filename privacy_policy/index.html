Privacy Policy for Spirit Echo

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application Spirit Echo (“Application”), developed by Nikita Rysin Studio (“Service Provider”). The Application is provided as a Freemium service and is intended for use AS IS.

1. Information We Collect

The Application may collect certain types of data automatically for analytics, functionality, and in-app purchases. The data collected does not include personally identifiable information such as your name, email, or address.

a) Usage Data

Collected via Unity Analytics:

  • application interactions (in-app actions, screens visited)
  • device information (device model, OS version, language)
  • IP-based approximate location (country only)
  • session lengths, performance metrics

b) Crash Logs & Diagnostics

Collected via Unity services to help improve stability:

  • crash reports
  • performance diagnostics
  • technical logs

c) In-App Purchase Information

When you make purchases, the following data may be processed:

  • purchase history (items purchased)
  • transaction identifiers
  • payment confirmation from Google Play Billing

This data is used solely to deliver purchased content and prevent fraud.

d) Device Identifiers

Unity Analytics may collect device identifiers such as:

  • Unity device ID
  • Advertising ID (if applicable)

2. How We Use This Information

  • To analyze how the Application is used
  • To improve gameplay, balance, UI, and overall experience
  • To fix bugs and improve stability
  • To process and validate in-app purchases

3. Third-Party Service Providers

The Application uses the following third-party services, which may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Unity Analytics
  • Unity IAP / Unity Services
  • Google Play Billing

No data is sold or shared with third parties for advertising or profiling.

4. Children's Privacy

The Application is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe that a child provided us with data, please contact us at the email below.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. All transmitted data (such as analytics and purchase verification) is sent securely using encrypted connections.

6. Your Rights

You may stop data collection at any time by uninstalling the Application.

Users in the EU and certain regions may have additional rights regarding data access, deletion, and restriction under GDPR.
